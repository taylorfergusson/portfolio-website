About Taylor Fergusson

Hello! I'm Taylor, a recent McGill graduate with a background in computer science, psychology, and sociology. I specialize at merging techncial expertise with an understanding of human behaviour, applying this to my personal projects, UX design endeavours, and coding instruction. As a coding instructor, I teach programming fundamentals to children and young adults, using interactive lessons and engaging delivery to promote computational thinking and excitement for computer science. I'm currently working on strengthening my UX design skills, using my interests in music, digital art, and accessibility to create engaging and interactive programs. With a passion for technology, psychology, and art, along with a human-focused perspective that motivates me to put user needs at the forefront, I'm devoted to strengthening the relationship between humans and computers by making technology more accessible to diverse populations.
