About Taylor Fergusson

Hello! As a recent McGill graduate with a background in computer science and psychology, I excel at blending technical expertise with an understanding of human behaviour. Professionally, as a coding instructor, I specialize in simplifying complex concepts for young students, helping them build strong foundations in coding, and igniting their interest in technology by creating an atmosphere of optimism and enthusiasm around the subject. With a passion for integrating technology and psychology, I’m dedicated to creating projects and innovative solutions with the user’s perspective at the forefront, and I'm excited to explore new opportunities and contribute to endeavors that push the boundaries of both fields.
