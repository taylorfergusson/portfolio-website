COMING SOON: FoundCloud Finder

A specialized music recognition program that uses advanced neural networks and spectrogram analysis to identify songs from SoundCloud in audio clips. Driven by the wish to catalog underground songs after discovering them in DJ mixes, FoundCloud matches short audio clips with songs in its database, regardless of distortion, noise, pitch alteration, or tempo adjustments.
